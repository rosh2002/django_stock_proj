This web page consists of two things, lets get familiar with it first.
1) The "Get Stock Quotes" Section : You maybe able to see a search bar at the top , with a Search button in green besides. This will help you in searching for a stock using the "ticker symbol". It will fetch details like the company name, stock price, previous close, market cap, year-to-date change, 52week high and 52 week low.

Note : A ticker is a symbol of a stock. It's a set of characters below 5 in count, that are used to represent that company's stock on the stock market.
